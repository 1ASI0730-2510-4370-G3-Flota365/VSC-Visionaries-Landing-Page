About

Meet our company

Flota365 is a comprehensive fleet management solution designed to help companies of all sizes manage their vehicles more efficiently. Our platform centralizes all aspects of fleet management, from maintenance scheduling and fuel tracking to document management and driver supervision.

With over 10 years of experience in the fleet management industry, we understand the challenges fleet managers face daily. That's why we've created a solution that is both powerful and easy to use, allowing you to focus on your business while we take care of your fleet.

Our mission is to reduce operational costs, improve vehicle lifespan, and enhance driver safety through intelligent fleet management. Join thousands of satisfied customers who have transformed their fleet operations with Flota365.

Features

Learn about the features of our software

Maintenance

Schedule and track all maintenance activities, receive alerts for upcoming services, and maintain complete service history for each vehicle.

Vehicle Checklists

Create customized inspection checklists, perform digital vehicle inspections, and identify issues before they become problems.

Fuel Management

Track fuel consumption, analyze efficiency, identify unusual fuel usage patterns, and integrate with fuel cards for automatic data collection.

Document Management

Store and manage all documents related to vehicles, drivers, and providers in one secure location with automatic expiration alerts.

Tire Management

Monitor tire conditions, track rotations and replacements, and optimize tire life to reduce costs and improve safety.

Purchases and Inventory

Manage parts inventory, track purchases, set reorder points, and ensure you always have the parts you need when you need them.

Trip and Advance Management

Plan and track trips, manage advance payments, and monitor travel expenses for better budget control and optimization.
